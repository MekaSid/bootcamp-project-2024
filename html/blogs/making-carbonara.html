To make authentic Carbonara, start by boiling your pasta in salted water. While the pasta cooks, sauté diced pancetta in olive oil until crispy. In a bowl, whisk together eggs, Pecorino Romano cheese, and a generous amount of black pepper. Once the pasta is cooked, reserve some pasta water and drain. Quickly toss the pasta with the pancetta, then stir in the egg mixture, adding reserved pasta water as needed to create a silky sauce. Serve immediately with extra cheese and pepper.
